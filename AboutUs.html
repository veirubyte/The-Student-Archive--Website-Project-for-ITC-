About Us

The developers of The Student Archive are first-year IT students at Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges. As current students, the developers thought about how great it would be if there was a dedicated site solely for helping students in terms of suggesting helpful academic videos, recommending student-friendly gadgets and items, and other school-related materials. Because of that, The Student Archive is created.

    The Student Archive is a site built by students for students. It offers many great services namely:
  • Showing timely trending news and happenings in the academic setting.
  • Providing helpful study videos and tips.
  • Showcasing student must-haves for studying.
  • Recommending the latest books.
  • Suggesting gadgets that are student-friendly in terms of price without sacrificing value.
